About Us

Handmade crochet & knit pieces

Amiguruxmi is a small business specializing in handcrafted crochet and knit products, made with love and attention to detail. From stylish accessories to adorable amigurumi toys, each item is carefully designed to bring warmth and happiness into your life.

Using high-quality and natural materials and a passion for creativity, Amiguruxmi offers unique, one-of-a-kind pieces perfect for gifting or treating yourself. Every stitch tells a story, making each product a heartfelt treasure.
